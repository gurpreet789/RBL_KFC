Pizza Hut prepares a large volume of fresh pizza dough in store, all of which is made from wheat flour containing gluten and all of which are prepared on the same make table, are cooked on the same pans, in the same oven and are also cut using the same board and knife. While Pizza Hut takes due care to prevent the cross contamination of ingredients and bases, because of the nature of our preparation and cooking areas we cannot guarantee that our pizzas are gluten free.

Some people have special dietary needs and suffer from a gluten allergy, intolerance or coeliac disease. Levels of dietary sensitivity to gluten and the seriousness of these reactions in these individuals may vary depending on the type of sensitivity and the individual involved. It is therefore highly recommended that if you are unsure about consuming one of our pizzas due to a gluten sensitivity that you take your personal dietary sensitivity into account with the knowledge that we cannot guarantee that any of our pizzas are totally gluten free (even if made on a gluten free base).
